Halloween Hangman

Welcome!

The rules of this Halloween themed Hangman game are simple. Press any key on the keyboard to submit a guess. If your guess corresponds to a letter in the word it will be revealed. If you can figure out the word before the amount of guesses runs out you win, but watch out as each incorrect guess will count agaisnt you! If the number of remaining guesses reaches zero you lose.
